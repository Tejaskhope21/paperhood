Orders are typically processed and printed within 24 hours of confirmation. Standard domestic shipping takes 3–5 business days; express options are available for 1–2 day delivery. International shipping to 50+ countries takes 7–14 business days depending on destination. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.
We offer white and cream interior paper in 70gsm, 80gsm, and 100gsm weights. For covers, choose from soft cover (matte or gloss laminate) or hardcover (case-bound). Binding options include perfect binding, saddle-stitch (for shorter books), and case binding. Pro and Publisher plan customers can also request custom paper samples before ordering.
Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If your books arrive with printing defects, damage, or don't match your approved proof, we will reprint and reship at no cost to you.
