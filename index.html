FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about printing with Paperhood

What file formats do you accept?
We accept PDF, DOCX, EPUB, MOBI, and InDesign (INDD) files. For best results, we recommend uploading a print-ready PDF with embedded fonts and 300 DPI images.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
No minimum order! Print one copy or thousands. We believe authors should have the freedom to print exactly what they need, when they need it.
How long does printing and shipping take?
Standard production takes 5-7 business days. Rush production (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. Shipping times vary by location (3-10 business days internationally).
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 120 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
Can I order a sample before printing in bulk?
Absolutely! Order a single proof copy at production cost to check quality, paper, and binding before committing to larger quantities.
What binding options are available?
We offer perfect binding (softcover), smyth-sewn binding (hardcover), spiral binding, and saddle stitching for booklets.
